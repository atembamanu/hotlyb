About Hotlyb

At Hotylyb, we all come to work every day because we want to bridge the gap between techie companies and the universities . Every student is guessing. Studnets don’t know what careers to venture in, what programmimg languages to code with, what solutions to make, how to monetize them, or even what to price them at. They also don't know what is expected of them at the job market, what technologies are used, what knowledge to posses, and how fast they should grow.In the same note, some have great ideas, yet they do not no how to make the ideas into realization. These Awesome ideas do not get nurtured, and they never grow.

Mission

Our Mission is to make HotLyb the best online School when it comes to supporting students achieve the best, be job competitive and problem solvers, not just graduates. Making this platform the best platform to ensure every student receives the best knowledge set and skills to make them employed.

Vision

Information technology is a foundation for designing an efficient business and also ensuring that the business model evolves with the technological renovation. Our vision revolves around "Exploring the creative solutions and advancements in the technologies and realizing the opportunities of the digital world and passing this opportunities to every student in the country ensuring upon completion of curriculum they can successfuly get employed."
